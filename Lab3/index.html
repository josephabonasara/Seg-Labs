Dewi's Groceries

Here are the instructions on how to successfully use Dewi's Groceries:

1- You can start in the Client tab where you will be asked to choose your preferences between 3 options; Organic, Lactose-free and/or Nut-free.

2- Once you completed this step you can press the Next button to go to the Products page.

3- You can select what products you want on the products page by pressing on the checkbox over the image. Once finished, you can press the "Make my cart!" button.

4- You will be taken to the Cart page where you will see all the products you selected.

5- Finally, you can purchase these products by pressing the "Proceed to Checkout" button, or you can go back and update your cart by pressing the "Go back to products" button.
